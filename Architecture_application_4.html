ATLANTIS, A SECRET COVE IN IBIZA
Fig: ATLANTIS, A SECRET COVE IN IBIZA

              With its huge and almost geometrically-perfect rocks, Atlantis – Ibiza’s hidden cove – is one of the most read and talked-about places you’ll hear about when it comes to the island’s hidden secrets.The Ratio of its length to breadth is approximately equal to 1.618 (Golden Ratio).
